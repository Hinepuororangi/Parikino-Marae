Tangihanga

There is no set charge for tangihanga — the marae operates on a koha basis, and we will walk alongside your whānau. On arrival, the marae provides a koha toward your whānau's first kai. Grants are available through Ngā Tāngata Tiaki and Ātihau.

Kawa & Tikanga

Staying at the marae

  • Zero tolerance: illegal drugs, alcohol and fighting are strictly prohibited on the marae.
  • No smoking or vaping inside any buildings.
  • Please keep all areas of the marae tidy during your stay.
  • No driving on the marae — please use the designated parking areas.

If you need support with a pōwhiri or tangihanga, let us know in your booking and we will guide you and your whānau. Take some time for the awa — Parikino is well known for its fruit and fishing spots.
